Governance

Is there a formal governance mechanism in place that meets, as a minimum, the requirements outlined below?

  1. The governance mechanism is formally recognised by seniour leadership of the care system.
  2. The governance mechanism is represented on, or has access to, the care system's board (or equivalent very senior mechanism).
  3. Is the governance mechanism is regularly attended by each organisation that has current or planned responsibility for data management or information governance relating to PHM within the care system?
  4. Is the governance mechanism attended by members with sufficient seniority, authority and subject matter expertise in relation to information governance and data managment? (e.g. Data Protection Officers or Chief Information/Data Officers)?
